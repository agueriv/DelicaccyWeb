PRIVATE EVENTS

At Delicaccy, we pride ourselves on offering an exceptional service for private events, specially designed to make your special occasion an unforgettable experience.

Our gourmet restaurant offers an elegant and sophisticated atmosphere, ideal for exclusive celebrations. From the décor to the personalised menu, we ensure that every aspect of your event reflects your style and preferences.

To book our private event service, simply complete the contact form on our website. We will contact you as soon as possible to discuss the details of your event and provide you with a personalised quote.

We look forward to the opportunity to be part of your special occasion at Delicaccy!
