OUR HISTORY

discover

In 2010, Delicaccy, a Michelin-starred restaurant founded by the Martínez brothers, opened in Granada. Noted for its focus on signature cuisine, it fuses avant-garde techniques with high quality ingredients. Located in a historic building, it offers a unique dining experience with creative and exquisite dishes. Since its opening, it has been recognised for its culinary excellence and has been awarded several Michelin stars. Delicaccy has become a must-visit destination for lovers of haute cuisine in search of an unforgettable experience.

THE OWNERS

know

Siblings Marta and Javier Martínez had always had a passion for gastronomy. From a young age, they experimented in their home kitchen and dreamed of one day opening their own restaurant. After studying at cooking school and working in some of the best restaurants in Spain, the brothers decided to join forces to create something truly unique. In 2010, they opened Delicaccy restaurant in Granada. Their focus on signature cuisine allowed them to create unique dishes that delighted diners.

Delicaccy's culinary excellence did not go unnoticed. Soon, the restaurant received its first Michelin star, and then another, and another, and another. Over time, Delicaccy has become a must-visit destination for lovers of haute cuisine. The Martínez brothers dedicate themselves to leading the restaurant with passion and dedication, offering their customers an unparalleled culinary experience. Their vision and talent allowed them to create something truly special in Granada, and their legacy will live on in the history of Spanish gastronomy.
