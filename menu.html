MALLARD DUCK

Foi gras and duck meat

The Royal Duck Delight with Ecstasy Glaze is a culinary masterpiece from Delicaccy that combines the richness of foie gras and the juiciness of duck meat in a sweet sauce that will make you salivate. The dish begins with a bed of wild rice, on which is placed a generous portion of duck meat roasted to perfection and accompanied by a layer of foie gras. The duck meat melts in the mouth, while the foie gras adds a smooth, creamy texture to the dish

The sweet sauce that covers the dish is a perfect combination of sweet and sour flavours, with a hint of honey and ginger that perfectly complements the duck meat and foie gras. The glaze is spread all over the dish, creating a delicious symphony of flavours in every bite

In short, the Royal Duck Delight with Ecstasy Glaze is an exquisite dish that combines gourmet ingredients in a perfect combination. If you are a lover of sophisticated and tasty food, this culinary delight from Delicaccy is not to be missed!

SUMMER CHICKEN

Grilled chicken with bulgur

Delicaccy's Summer Flavoured Farmhouse Delight is a dish that combines the juiciness of grilled chicken fillet with the soft, slightly crunchy texture of bulgur, and the freshness of a selection of seasonal vegetables.

The chicken fillet is cooked to perfection on the grill, sealing in the juices and adding a smoky flavour that makes it irresistible. The bulgur, meanwhile, is cooked just right to maintain its crunchy texture and blended with aromatic herbs such as coriander and cumin, giving it a delicious flavour and heady aroma.

The selection of fresh vegetables includes cherry tomatoes, cucumbers, red onion and capsicum, which add a crisp and refreshing touch to the dish. The combination of flavours and textures creates a perfect symphony in every bite.

In short, the Summer Aroma Farmhouse Delight is a delicious and healthy option that combines fresh and tasty ingredients in a perfect combination. If you're looking for a dish that makes you feel good both inside and out, this culinary delight from Delicaccy is not to be missed!
