JAVIER
MARTÍNEZ

Head chef

Javier Martinez always knew that his passion was cooking. From a young age, he would help his mother in the kitchen at home, experimenting with different ingredients and flavours. Eventually, he realised that he wanted to take his love of food to a professional level.

After graduating from culinary school, Javier worked in various restaurants in Spain and France, honing his skills and gaining experience. But he always knew he wanted to have his own restaurant one day. In 2012, he received his first Michelin star, confirming his place as one of Spain's top chefs.

But Javier's real inspiration was his sister Marta, who battled cancer for several years. Despite the difficulties, Marta always kept a positive attitude and never lost her love for life and food.

“I will always fight for our dream and for my career with the courage and strength that my sister did.”

Says Javier, 2012

OUR CUISINE TEAM

about

The Delicaccy kitchen team is a highly skilled and committed group, led by head chef Javier Martinez. Working alongside him is his sister, chef Marta Martinez, who brings her experience and skills in the preparation of fresh, high quality ingredients. Completing the team is second chef, Asanne Diop, who is known for his enthusiasm and dedication

The Delicaccy kitchen runs like a well-oiled machine, with each member of the team playing a critical role in creating exceptional dishes. Javier leads the team with his creativity and culinary skills, while Marta works closely with him to ensure efficiency and quality in the preparation of ingredients. Asanne supports Javier and Marta in the preparation of dishes and in the cleaning and organisation of the kitchen.

Together, this highly skilled and dedicated team works tirelessly to provide an exceptional culinary experience for Delicaccy's customers.
